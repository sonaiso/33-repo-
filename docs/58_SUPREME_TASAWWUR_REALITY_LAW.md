## Radical Correction

> Reference (الإحالة) is not merely a layer after the carrier —
> it is the **condition of possibility** for the carrier.
>
> Before saying "this is an identity-preserved carrier," there must be
> an effect that has been referred to a probable source.
> Without pre-conceptual reference, no carrier appears at all —
> effects remain scattered without unity.

---

## Declaration

> The Supreme Reference-Tasawwur Law establishes that judgment (حكم) does not begin
> from language, nor from the carrier, nor even from cognition — it begins from:
>
> **Reality (واقع) → Effect (أثر) → Need/Attention (افتقار/تنبه) → Pre-conceptual Reference (إحالة قبلية) → Conception (تصور) → Carrier (حامل)**
>
> The key insight: **Reference opens conception, conception opens the carrier, and the carrier preserves identity.**

---

## Two Types of Reference (الإحالة نوعان)

### a. Pre-conceptual Reference (الإحالة القبلية)

Before complete language and before judgment.

**Function**: Effect → Probable source → Candidate carrier / transformation / linking.

Examples:
- Heat effect → reference to a probable heat source
- Sound effect → reference to a probable sonic event
- Writing effect → reference to a probable written signifier
- Motion effect → reference to a probable kinetic transformation

This reference does not give judgment — it opens the possibility of conception.

### b. Discourse Reference (الإحالة الخطابية)

Inside speech, after utterances and compositions exist.

**Function**: Pronoun / demonstrative / relative / context → referent within a discourse domain.

Example: "جاء زيد، وهو مسرور" — "هو" refers discursively to "زيد".

**Law**:
- Pre-conceptual reference is the condition of possibility for the carrier.
- Discourse reference is the condition of regularity for the carrier within speech.

## The 29 Layers

### Layer 0 — Reality Layer (طبقة الواقع)

```text
واقع كيان / واقع تحول / واقع ربط / واقع أثر
```

Reality here does not mean we have judged it — it means the system presupposes
a domain from which effects can originate.

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| واقع كيان (Entity-reality) | Stone, fire, human |
| واقع تحول (Transformation-reality) | Motion, heat, writing |
| واقع ربط (Linking-reality) | Causation, adjacency, sequence |
| واقع أثر (Effect-reality) | Sound, light, heat, inscription, movement |

**Law**: Reality preserves existence. But judgment on reality comes only later, with evidence.

---

### Layer 1 — Effect Transfer Layer (طبقة انتقال الأثر)

```text
أثر ينتقل من الواقع أو من دعوى الواقع إلى جهاز صالح للتلقي.
```

The effect may be: phonetic, visual, thermal, kinetic, written, digital, or memorial.

**Law**:
- No attention without effect.
- No considered effect without a domain of reception.

---

### Layer 2 — Receptive Brain Layer (طبقة الجهاز المتلقي)

```text
تلقي، تمييز، حفظ، مقارنة، استدعاء.
```

| Operation | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| تلقي (Reception) | Entry of the effect |
| تمييز (Discrimination) | Isolating it from others |
| حفظ (Retention) | Preserving its trace |
| مقارنة (Comparison) | Linking it to prior traces |
| استدعاء (Recall) | Summoning stored traces |

This is not intellectual judgment yet — it is receptive capacity.

**Law**: An effect does not become conception merely by arriving. It needs
discrimination, retention, and comparison.

---

### Layer 3 — Iftiqar-Attention Layer (طبقة الافتقار والتنبه)

```text
افتقار يفتح التنبه، والتنبه يطلب الإحالة.
```

The intellect does not begin from ready-made information, but from a gap:
- What is this?
- Where did this effect come from?
- What is its source?
- Is it an entity? A transformation? A linking?

**Law**:
- No reference without need.
- No conception without a request for reference.

---

### Layer 4 — Pre-Conceptual Reference Layer (طبقة الإحالة قبل التصور)

```text
أثر → مصدر محتمل / حامل محتمل / تحول محتمل / ربط محتمل
```

Here begins the first reference:

| Effect | Candidate reference |
|--------|---------------------|
| Heard sound | Reference to a probable human sound |
| Visible drawing | Reference to a probable letter |
| Sensed heat | Reference to a probable heat source |
| Visible motion | Reference to a probable kinetic transformation |

**Law**: Pre-conceptual reference does not establish the origin. It nominates a candidate origin.

---

### Layer 5 — Tasawwur Layer (طبقة التصور)

```text
تصور حامل / تصور تحول / تصور ربط / تصور أثر
```

Conception here is not judgment — it is the construction of a first reasonable image:
- This may be a stone.
- This may be writing.
- This may be an Arabic sound.
- This may be a causal relation.

All are candidate conceptions, not final judgments.

**Law**: Conception nominates a basis (مناط). It does not judge the basis.

---

### Layer 6 — Prior Classified Knowledge Layer (طبقة المعرفة السابقة المصنفة)

```text
معلومات سابقة عن الكيان والحقيقة والخواص والتحولات والربط والأصوات والحروف والأوزان.
```

After conception, the intellect summons its prior knowledge.

**Law**:
- No classification without prior knowledge.
- Prior knowledge is not fit for judgment unless classified and domain-bounded.

---

### Layer 7 — Carrier-Identity Layer (طبقة الحامل والهوية)

```text
حامل يحفظ الهوية داخل مجال إحالة.
```

The carrier does not appear until effects have been referred back to a unity that preserves them.

| Effects | Candidate carrier |
|---------|-------------------|
| Hardness + extension + weight | Stone |
| Heat + light + burning | Fire |
| Ordered phonetic effects | Utterance |

**Laws**:
- No carrier without reference.
- No carrier without preserved identity.

---

### Layer 8 — Genus Layer (طبقة الجنس)

```text
الجنس يصنف الهوية.
```

After the carrier, genus classifies its identity.

---

### Layer 9 — Essence Layer (طبقة الحقيقة)

```text
الحقيقة تثبت ما به الشيء هو هو.
```

Essence establishes "what makes it what it is."

**Law**:
- Essence prevents the entity from dissolving into its attributes.
- Properties prevent bearing an attribute that its genus cannot support.

---

### Layer 10 — Properties Layer (طبقة الخواص)

```text
الخواص تكشف القابليات.
```

Properties reveal the carrier's capacities — what it can bear and what it cannot.

Example (stone): hardness, weight, breakability, ownership-capacity, annexation-capacity.
Example (human): intention, understanding, writing, speech, ownership, learning.

---

### Layer 11 — Attribute-Transformation Layer (طبقة الصفة ومصدر التحول)

```text
الصفة تختبر قابلية الخواص. والمصدر يرد الصفة إلى أصل تحول.
```

An attribute is not borne upon an entity directly. Rather:

```text
Entity → Properties
Attribute → Transformation source
Then test: Do the entity's properties support the demands of the transformation source?
```

Example:
- كاتب (writer): source = كتابة (writing); demands = agency, intention or instrument, writable object
- Human + كاتب = reasonable
- Pen + كاتب = reasonable (instrumentally)
- Stone + كاتب = blocked (unless by context/evidence)

**Law**:
- No attribute without a transformation source.
- No bearing of an attribute without testing the carrier's properties.

---

### Layer 12 — Source Transformation Layer (طبقة مصدر التحول وعلاقاته)

```text
فاعلية، مفعولية، سببية، مسببية، مطاوعة، مشاركة، تكرار، مكان، زمان، آلة.
```

After the source, the transformational relations appear:

| Relation | Question |
|----------|----------|
| فاعلية (Agency) | Does it have an agent? |
| مفعولية (Patiency) | Does it fall on a locus? |
| سببية (Causation) | Is the carrier the cause? |
| مسببية (Being caused) | Is the carrier the effect? |
| مطاوعة (Receptivity) | Did it accept an effect? |
| مشاركة (Participation) | Does it require two parties? |
| تكرار (Repetition) | Is it iterative? |
| مكان (Place) | Does it require a location? |
| زمان (Time) | Does it require a time? |
| آلة (Instrument) | Does it require a tool? |

**Law**: The source reveals the demands of the transformation. No demand may be imposed on a carrier that cannot support it.

---

### Layer 13 — Weight Witness Layer (طبقة الوزن الشاهد)

```text
الوزن يشهد على فرع مرخص ولا يخلق الأصل.
```

The correct order:

```text
Transformation source → Licensed branch → Weight witness
```

Never: `Weight → Meaning`.

Examples:
- فاعل witnesses an agency-branch from a transformation source
- مفعول witnesses a patiency-branch from a transformation source
- حجري witnesses an attribution-branch from a static entity

**Law**:
- No licensed weight without an origin.
- No licensed origin without reference.
- No licensed reference without a domain.

---

### Layer 14 — Operator-Linking Layer (طبقة الأداة والربط)

```text
أداة تضبط الربط والمنع والشرط والسبب والإحالة والترتيب.
```

The operator is not an entity nor an attribute. Its origin is:
linking, blocking, conditioning, causing, excepting, restricting, referring, sequencing.

| Operator | Gate |
|----------|------|
| و | AND |
| أو | OR |
| لا / لم / لن | NOT |
| إن / إذا | IF-THEN |
| لأن / كي | CAUSE |
| إلا | EXCEPTION |
| إنما / لا...إلا | ONLY-IF |
| هذا / الذي | REFERENCE |
| ثم / فـ | SEQUENCE |
| من / إلى | START/END |
| في / على | CONTAINMENT/SUPERIORITY |

**Law**: The operator governs linking. It does not produce judgment alone.

### Layer 15 — Phono-Lafz Layer (طبقة الدال واللفظ)

```text
الدال الصوتي/الكتابي/الرقمي يغلق مادة اللفظ.
```

Three paths must be distinguished:

**a. Natural phonetic path (المسار الصوتي الطبيعي)**:
```text
Physical sonic event → Sound → Human sound → Arabic sound
→ Phoneme/vowel mark → Articulation point and features → Syllable → Utterance
```

**b. Written path (المسار الكتابي)**:
```text
Visual effect → Drawing → Written form → Letter/vowel mark
→ Lexical unit → Utterance
```

**c. Digital path (المسار الرقمي)**:
```text
Code Point / Unicode → Digital symbol → Glyph / visible form
→ Letter or vowel mark in writing system → Written signifier unit → Readable utterance
```

**Important**: Unicode is not the ontological origin of the letter. It is a digital reference
to a written symbol. The written symbol refers to a letter/vowel mark within a script system.
The letter/vowel mark refers to a phonetic/morphological function by domain.

**Law**: The signifier opens the utterance. The utterance does not close unless its
phonetic/written units and domain are determined.

### Layer 16 — Singular Closure Layer (طبقة إقفال المفرد)

```text
إقفال المفرد: كيان / تحول / أداة / علم / مبني / دخيل / منقول / مصدر / صفة.
```

Singular closure is the condition of possibility for the word.

Before an utterance enters composition, it must be closed as a singular:
- Is it an entity?
- Is it a transformation?
- Is it an operator?
- Is it a proper name?
- Is it indeclinable?
- Is it foreign?
- Is it transferred?
- Is it a source/masdar?
- Is it an attribute?

**Law**:
- No word fit for composition without singular closure.
- No singular closure without determining its path: carrier / transformation / linking.

---

### Layer 17 — Word Contract Layer (طبقة الكلمة)

```text
المفرد المغلق يصير كلمة: اسم / فعل / حرف.
```

| Word type | Condition of possibility |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| الاسم (Noun) | Carrier, attribute, masdar, proper name, or meaning capable of predication/annexation |
| الفعل (Verb) | Transformation bound to time, aspect, or predication |
| الحرف (Particle) | Linking operator without independent carrier-capacity |

The word does not function alone — it enters a relation:
governor/governed, subject/predicate, annexor/annexed, qualifier/qualified, etc.

**Law**: The word is the condition of possibility for composition. Composition is the condition of possibility for relation.

---

### Layer 18 — Composition Layer (طبقة التركيب)

```text
جملة اسمية / جملة فعلية / شبه جملة.
```

**a. Nominal sentence (الجملة الاسمية)**:
Subject + predicate → bearing an attribute or report upon a carrier.
Example: "الجبل صلب" (The mountain is solid).

**b. Verbal sentence (الجملة الفعلية)**:
Verb + agent + patient/complements → transformation attributed to an agent or falling upon a patient.
Example: "كتب الرجل الرسالة" (The man wrote the letter).

**c. Semi-sentence (شبه الجملة)**:
Preposition+governed / adverb → spatial, temporal, or relational reference.
Example: "في البيت" (in the house), "عند الباب" (at the door).

**Law**: Composition patterns are the condition of possibility for relations:
predicative, inclusive, restrictive, causal, patiency, adverbial, annexive.

---

### Layer 19 — Syntactic Relation Layer (طبقة النسب التركيبية)

```text
عامل ومعمول، مسند ومسند إليه، فاعل ومفعول، إضافة وتقييد.
```

After composition, relations appear:

| Relation | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| إسنادية (Predicative) | Bearing a predicate/verb upon a carrier |
| تضمينية (Inclusive) | Embedding meaning within meaning |
| تقييدية (Restrictive) | Narrowing the domain of carrier/attribute |
| فاعلية (Agency) | Attributing verb to agent |
| مفعولية (Patiency) | Verb falling upon patient |
| سببية (Causation) | Linking verb to cause |
| مسببية (Being caused) | Linking effect to prior cause |
| ظرفية (Adverbial) | Spatial/temporal containment |
| إضافية (Annexive) | Possession/specification relation |
| ملكية (Ownership) | Belonging relation |

**Law**: No communication without a closed relation. No relation without conceived parties.

---

### Layer 20 — I'rab Layer (طبقة الإعراب)

```text
علامات أصلية وفرعية شاهدة على العلاقات.
```

Case markings are not phonetic decoration — they are relation witnesses:

| Marking | Relation witnessed |
|---------|-------------------|
| الرفع (Nominative) | Predication / agency / subject-hood |
| النصب (Accusative) | Patiency / restriction / adverbial |
| الجر (Genitive) | Annexation / prepositional attachment |
| الجزم (Jussive) | Conditional/apodictic relation |

**Law**: I'rab is a relation witness. It does not determine meaning alone.

---

### Layer 21 — Discourse Reference Layer (طبقة الإحالة الخطابية)

```text
ضمير، إشارة، موصول، سياق، مقام.
```

After composition, discourse reference appears:
- Pronoun → referent
- Demonstrative → indicated entity
- Relative → connected clause
- Context → surrounding discourse
- Situation → circumstance of use

**Law**: Discourse reference binds the carrier within speech. It is the second type of reference (after pre-conceptual).

---

### Layer 22 — Style Reference Layer (طبقة الإحالة الأسلوبية)

```text
خبر، إنشاء، أمر، نهي، استفهام، شرط، قسم، تعجب، تمنٍّ، ترجٍّ.
```

After discourse reference, stylistic reference appears — determining the type of use:

| Style | Reference type |
|-------|---------------|
| الخبر (Declarative) | Reference to truth/falsity capability |
| الإنشاء (Performative) | Reference to request/command/prohibition |
| الاستفهام (Interrogative) | Reference to unknown requiring answer |
| الشرط (Conditional) | Reference to contingent dependency |
| القسم (Oath) | Reference to emphasis/verification |
| التعجب (Exclamation) | Reference to exceeding expectation |

**Law**: Context (المقام) licenses the type of use. It does not establish judgment alone.

---

### Layer 23 — Mantooq Layer (طبقة المنطوق)

```text
إحالة داخل حد القول.
```

The explicit (المنطوق) is what the utterance closed within the boundary of saying.

**Law**: No mafhum without a preserved mantooq.

---

### Layer 24 — Mafhoom Layer (طبقة المفهوم)

```text
إحالة فرعية خارج حد القول.
```

The implied (المفهوم) is what is understood outside the boundary of the explicit,
by licensed branch-derivation.

**Law**:
- No mafhum without a preserved mantooq.
- No mafhum without an origin (أصل).
- No origin without branch-capacity (فرعية).
- No branch-capacity without an inspected preventer (مانع مفحوص).

---

### Layer 25 — Ma'qool Layer (طبقة المعقول)

```text
إحالة عقلية إلى لازم مضبوط.
```

The rational (المعقول) is what conception, relation, and evidence accept as a
controlled entailment.

**Law**:
- No rational meaning without reference and domain.
- No judgment from mafhum or ma'qool without evidence.

---

### Layer 26 — Ifadah Layer (طبقة الإفادة)

```text
إغلاق النسبة بين تصورات معقولة.
```

Communication (إفادة) is the closure of a relation between reasonable conceptions:

```text
Conceived carrier + Conceived attribute/transformation + Licensed linking = Communication
```

Example: "الجبل صلب" — bearing solidity upon the mountain — is communicative because:
- الجبل = conceived carrier
- صلب = conceived attribute with known transformation source
- The relation = bearing the attribute upon the carrier

But it does not become a factual judgment until evidence stands.

**Law**: Communication is the condition of possibility for judgment. It is not the judgment itself.

---

### Layer 27 — Hukm Layer (طبقة الحكم)

```text
حكم بدليل.
```

Types of judgment: linguistic, syntactic, morphological, semantic, factual, legal, computational.

Every judgment requires:
- A claim (دعوى)
- A domain (مجال)
- Evidence (دليل)
- A rank (رتبة)
- A condition (شرط)
- A potential preventer (مانع)
- Declared residuals (بقايا)

**Law**:
- No judgment without evidence.
- No evidence without domain.
- No domain without rank.
- No rank without residuals.

---

### Layer 28 — Tanzil Layer (طبقة التنزيل)

```text
تنزيل بتحقيق مناط.
```

Application (تنزيل) is not general understanding — it is applying the judgment to a specific reality.

```text
Tanzil = Judgment + Specific reality + Verification of applicable basis (تحقيق مناط)
```

Example:
- General judgment: "This action causes X."
- Application: Does this specific action in this specific reality fulfill the basis of causation?

**Law**:
- No application without verification of the applicable basis.
- No verification without matching between judgment and specific reality.

## Graduated Closure Law (قانون الإقفال المتدرج)

> Note: This law describes 10 coarse-grained closure gates — major checkpoints
> in the 29-layer chain. Layers between gates are sub-steps within each gate's
> scope (e.g., CarrierClosure encompasses Layers 7-14; RelationClosure encompasses
> Layers 19-25). A gate does not close until all its constituent layers are satisfied.

```text
إقفال الأثر شرط إمكان التصور.
وإقفال التصور شرط إمكان الحامل.
وإقفال الحامل شرط إمكان المفرد.
وإقفال المفرد شرط إمكان الكلمة.
والكلمة شرط إمكان التركيب.
والتركيب شرط إمكان النسبة.
والنسبة شرط إمكان الإفادة.
والإفادة شرط إمكان الحكم.
والحكم شرط إمكان التنزيل.
```

Closure chain:

```text
EffectClosure
→ TasawwurClosure
→ CarrierClosure
→ LafzClosure
```
