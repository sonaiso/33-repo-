## Central Atomic Laws

- No haraka without carrier.
- No carrier without surface.
- No surface without bridge to form.
- No form-level movement without HarakaFunctionSlot.
- No HarakaFunctionSlot without ProofObject.
- No meaning from haraka.
- No i'rab from haraka before relation.
- No hukm from i'rab before ifadeh.

## Forbidden Shortcut Claims

- `C=1 => tool`
- `C=3 => root`
- `CVCVCV => verb`
- `fatha => accusative`
- `damma => nominative`
