## Coverage Computation Law

ComputedVerdict cannot be manually supplied.
Dashboard must be computed.
YAML may declare expected_verdict only.
MRK defaults cannot be all true.

Coverage closure requires positive + negative + residual coverage.

## Required Separation

Coverage artifacts must distinguish:
- expected_verdict
- computed_verdict
- test_result
